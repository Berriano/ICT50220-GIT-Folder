Note

A 'cookie' is a small file in your web browser. Cookies gathers data about your web browsing behaviour. Even though cookies do not access information stored on your computer, or any personal files, they do allow collection of identifiable information. Use of cookies allows a website to customise its structure to your needs and likings, as well as possibly serve ads to you while you are browsing the Internet.

76457401 / Dinga / shutterstock.com

If you have a web site that collects personal information you need to state the way in which the information is collected.

Tip

Should my Privacy Policy include a notification about 'cookies'?

Yes. A business Privacy Policy should include a 'cookie' notification section if you are gathering information via a web site.
