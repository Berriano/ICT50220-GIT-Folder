This topic covered the knowledge and skills needed to best secure a business against common cyber threats, keep your software updated, what are the threats from emails and texts, and understanding real time protection.

The topic also looked at how to secure a mobile or tablet device, understand private and public Wi-Fi, how to use secure connections, and how to protect accounts.

The last part of the topic gave you an understanding of the cyber.gov.au resources.
