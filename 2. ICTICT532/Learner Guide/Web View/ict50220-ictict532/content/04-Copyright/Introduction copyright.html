Copyright is an issue for any person or organisation that develops any original work. This can be from a person writing a poem, an organisation developing a new web site, or even this learning material.

Without the protection of copyright, the people and organisations that develop their original work would not do so because of the loss or prestige, recognition, and in lots of cases financial income.

Objectives

Key concepts for this topic:

  • laws surrounding copyright
  • what does copyright protect
  • granting and getting copyright permission
  • protecting copyright
  • information on the web site for permission
  • types of media file copyright
  • particular problems obtaining copyright permissions
  • copyright clearance.

In addition to the material provided you will also need to spend some time on further research of these concepts.
