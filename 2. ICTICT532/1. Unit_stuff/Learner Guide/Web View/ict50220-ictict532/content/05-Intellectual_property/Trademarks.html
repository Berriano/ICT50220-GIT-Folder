Your trademark, often called a brand, is your identity. It is the way you show your customers who you are.

It might be your corporate logo, a jingle you use in an advertisement, your business name painted on the side of your truck or your signage or packaging.

A trademark can also be any of the following things.

  • word (in a plain font or a 'fancy' font)
  • phrase
  • letter
  • number
  • logo
  • picture
  • aspect of packaging
  • or combination of the above...

A common misconception is that a trademark is the same thing as a business name, company name or domain name. It is not.
