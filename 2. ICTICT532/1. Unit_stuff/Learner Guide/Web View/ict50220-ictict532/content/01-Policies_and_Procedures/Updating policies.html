A policy document clearly states an organisation's intentions. In the case of code of ethics policy, this is a set of statements about appropriate and expected behaviour of members of a company or a professional group, and as such, reflects the values of the company.

Copyright, intellectual property, privacy, and a code of ethics policy should be written and updated to incorporate any relevant legislation and industry standards.

Note

As mentioned previously, copyright and intellectual property must have alignment with the laws and legislations. In business, the word alignment is used to describe the process of developing systems to specifically support the needs of the company.

Example

We design a new website to address the clearly defined business needs of the company. By doing this, the organisation can make sure that the resources that go into deploying technology will be effective in helping the company meet its goals.
