Policies and procedures should be made available to all employees of the organisation and other stakeholders.

Policy and procedure documents are usually a matter of public record and signify that the company understands the importance of privacy, copyright, intellectual property, and ethical behaviour in their industry and the relevance of legislation and industry standards to the development of these policies.

Activity

Consider some of the ways that you could make a code of ethics available to the employees in a medium size organisation:

  • What technologies could be used?
  • How would you inform employees of the existence of the code of ethics?
  • How would you make sure that they have read it?
