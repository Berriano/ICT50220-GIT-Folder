Policies and procedure documents are two different categories of the functions of an organisation. Policies are the guidelines that govern the organisation's behaviour and processes. Procedures are the processes used to implement policies within the organisation.

A policy dictates what the standard or benchmark is, whereas, procedures guide policy changes, taking into account internal and external influences such as technology, workplace environment, and even state and federal legislation.

By having policies and procedures in place, you create standards and values for the organisation. They can also improve the way the customers and staff deal with your organisation. See below for more detail.
